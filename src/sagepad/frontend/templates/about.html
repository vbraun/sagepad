    About SagePad

    The goal of SagePad is a fast way to just try out some Sage commands. For now, it is mostly a technology demo and a testbed to explore secure execution of arbitrary code that is submitted via the internet.

    SagePad lets you use Sage in your browser, no installation requried. It is inspired by codepad, which lets you use a variety of programming languages (but not Sage, at least not yet).
